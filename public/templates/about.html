The Hattery:

Some Like it Hot (But Usually it's Bone-numbingly Cold)

Birdhaus helps the inhabitants of this space collect a comprehensive history of the temperature from three altitude-spaced temperature sensors

Technologies:

  • Data visualization via Highcharts helps users see what times of day are sweltering and which are akin to Arctic chill
  • Data is logged with three temperature sensors connected to a Raspberry Pi microcomputer that's been pushing readings directly to Heroku for the better part of a week
  • An AngularJS app build on the rock-solid foundation of Ruby on Rails
